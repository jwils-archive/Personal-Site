Ambiance (2011)

  • Awarded Second place of 30 teams and $1000 cash prize in PennApps Mobile Hackathon 2011.

Maplus (2010)

  • Third Place and Sponsored prize totaling $1000 cash in the PennApps Hackathon 2010.
